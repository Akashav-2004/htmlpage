Akash" can refer to several things, primarily: 1) a missile system, 2) a Sanskrit word, or 3) a coaching center. The Akash missile system is a medium-range surface-to-air missile developed by India. In Sanskrit, "Akash" (आकाश) translates to "sky" or "open air". Aakash is also the name of a leading coaching center in India, specializing in preparing students for medical and engineering entrance exams.
